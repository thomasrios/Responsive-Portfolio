About Me

Thomas is the greatest Madden player of all time. He began his journey at a young age and quickly learned he was a noob when he finally played online. He spent years getting beat so badly he wanted to cry but still persevered.

One day he decided to watch a professional player on youtube and learned the secrets to becoming a competitive player. He practiced the same plays over and over until he worked his way into the top division on Xbox for Madden Ultimate Team.
